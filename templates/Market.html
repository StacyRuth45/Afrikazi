About Kikoi

The kikoi, also known as a kikoy, is a traditional Swahili garment worn by both men and women in Kenya. It is a rectangular piece of woven fabric, typically 60 inches long and 30 inches wide. The kikoi is popular among tourists, who use it as a beach wrap or scarf.
